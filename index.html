Betrayle

Anger sounds like sirens crashing against your ears

It rings and rings inside your ears

Rage flows like lava through your veins

Boiling hot flows the pain

Riping and burning inside the brain

Booming through your waves of thought

Betrayle and hatrid reside inside

Feelings of abandon retch your eyes

Revenge so sweet as apple pie

Plans for vengence start to rise

Time to act on my villanouse upclimb
